About GreenB

GreenB is a small company set up by two bicycle fans. We have designed a bicycle in a way that is highly functional but yet both fashionable and colourful.

GreenB bicycle is very popular among commuters. Because the product is super light, it can be easily transported in trains and buses.

The uniqueness of the GreenB lies not in its ultra-lightness but also its fun for riding and oozes a new lifestyle. While the traditional battery-operated bikes are comparatively heavy and big and perceived as a dull transportation tool, the GreenB provides functions well beyond just a transportation tool but for greeness and sustainability. Because of its unique design, its users believe they are contributing to reduce negative human impact on our planet.

The GreenB can be used on pedals alone, on electric power alone, or combined. The ultra-small design of the bike makes it easy to be ridden on almost every occasion and in combination with other means of transportation. Most users ride it to commute to work.
